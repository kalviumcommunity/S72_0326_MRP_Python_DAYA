What is DAYA?

A data-driven system that forecasts hospital bed demand, identifies patterns, and provides actionable insights for healthcare administrators.

📈

Predictive Forecasting

7-14 day bed demand forecasts using moving averages and trend analysis to help with capacity planning.

🔍

Pattern Detection

Identifies seasonal trends, weekly patterns, and correlations between ICU admissions and bed occupancy.

⚠️

Smart Alerts

Automated alert system with warning (75th percentile) and critical (90th percentile) thresholds.

📊

Visual Analytics

9 professional visualizations including trends, distributions, forecasts, and comprehensive dashboards.

🧹

Data Processing

Cleaned 70K encounters, removed 41K invalid records, and engineered features for accurate analysis.

💡

Actionable Insights

Clear recommendations for staffing, resource allocation, and capacity management based on data.
